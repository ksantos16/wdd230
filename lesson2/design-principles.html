Design Principles Document

Visual Hierarchy

Verizon

verizon.com

Verizon's webpage is an example of Visual Hierarchy because of the bold typography to direct the eye on where it should go to.

Rule of Three

Coca-Cola

coca-cola.com

Coca-Cola's webpage is an example of Rule of Three because it has the navbar acting as the top, the photo the list of global names for the middle, and the footer with the copyrite as the bottom.

Contrast

Sony

electronics.sony.com

Sony's webpage is an example of Contrast because through out the webpage it has a constant play of light and dark from the photos to the lettering, and the background color.
